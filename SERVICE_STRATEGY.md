# Cybersecurity Service Strategy: From Labs to Market

## 🎯 Strategic Objective
To leverage automated technical auditing and criminological analysis to provide high-value security services for decentralized environments.

## 🛠️ Service Portfolio (6-Month Roadmap)

### 1. Linux Infrastructure Hardening (Launch: Month 1-2)
*   **Core Value:** Transforming audit findings into immediate system stability.
*   **Technical Focus:** Automated CIS Benchmark implementation via Python.
*   **Target:** SMEs with on-premise servers or specialized labs.

### 2. VIP Digital Hygiene (Launch: Month 3-4)
*   **Core Value:** Reducing the personal attack surface of high-profile targets.
*   **Technical Focus:** Account security auditing, MFA implementation, and data leak monitoring.
*   **Target:** Business executives and legal professionals.

### 3. Digital Evidence Preservation (Launch: Month 5-6)
*   **Core Value:** Ensuring forensic integrity during initial incident response.
*   **Technical Focus:** Secure log collection (Python-based) and Chain of Custody protocols.
*   **Strategic Advantage:** Combines a Criminology Degree with IT Operations expertise.
